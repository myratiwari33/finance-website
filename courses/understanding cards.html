What's a Card, Really?💳

A card = a magic rectangle for your money.

  • 💳 Debit card: spends *your* money
  • 🧾 Credit card: spends *borrowed* money
  • 📲 Works online & offline (swipe, tap, type)
  • 🛡️ Safer than carrying cash

It's like your wallet, but smarter.


Why YOU Should Care About Cards🧐

  • You can't always use cash, especially online
  • Debit = control spending. Credit = build score
  • Linked to UPI, apps, subscriptions & more
  • Using one smartly = grown-up level unlocked

Types of Cards (explained fast)🎴

🏷️ Card Type 🧠 Translation
Debit Spends what's in your bank
Credit Borrowed limit, pay later
Prepaid Recharge like a phone, then use
Virtual Online use only, no plastic

Interest & Credit = Use Smartly

  • ✅ Debit = no debt, no interest
  • ❌ Credit = free for a while, then charges if unpaid
  • 🧠 Tip: Pay credit bills *on time* to avoid traps

How to Get One (Simple!)📦

  1. Open a bank account first
  2. Request a debit card (usually free)
  3. For credit cards: apply if 18+, with income
  4. Activate and set PIN

Card Survival Tips🧷

  • Don't share card/PIN info
  • Set spending alerts on your app
  • Use virtual cards for safer online shopping
  • Read charges, especially with credit cards
